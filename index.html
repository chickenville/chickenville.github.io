About Chicken Ville

Chicken Invaders needs no introduction. Well, fine, if you insist. Chicken Invaders is the most successful chicken game of this millennium (not to mention the previous one). you at the forefront of a battle against invading intergalactic chickens, bent on revenge against the human race for our oppression of Earth chickens. In Chicken Ville, you assume the role of a new recruit in the United Hero Force (UHF), mankind's last hope against the fowl Henpire. You start your UHF career deployed in some backwater galactic star system, and it's up to you to advance through UHF ranks and earn your place in the honorary annals of Heroes Academy. Travel across the galaxy, explore strange new worlds, seek out new life and new civilizations, and exterminate any Henpire forces that cross your path. And do so in style.

    Future Plans

    Leaving Early Access will have practically no impact on the game’s continued development. Nevertheless, over the coming couple of months there won’t be any major content additions, because all effort will be concentrated on getting the mobile versions released. After the mobile versions are done, the development branch will most likely be split off into “stable” and “beta” branches for logistical reasons (or, alternatively, “rapid release” and “long-term support”). The details of this have still not been finalized, expect more soon. So, what else is there is store ? Hmm, let’s see…
